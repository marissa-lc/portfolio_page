Hello and welcome!
I am a recent graduate from the University of Oregon Full Stack Coding Bootcamp.
Getting into the coding world has been a complete transtition from the hospitality work I am used to and I have fallen in love with it.
To me coding feels a lot like making art; you have an idea of what you want to make in your head but putting it down on paper (or in this case in the computer) is a completely different story.
What you start with and what you end with are two incredibly different things, the trick is just starting somewhere and then continuing to add details until you can see what it's supposed to be.
Coding feels like a constant opportunity to create and evolve and it's something I am excited to continue to be a part of.
